Debut Album

Cumasc Gael are currently recording their first studio album.

'The Cutty Sark Mutiny' is a true reflection of the group's unique, 'Celtic-Bluegrass' sound. Featuring 11 foot-stomping modern classics including the singles: 'Gagged and Bound', 'G.I.G (God is Good) and 'I Said Nothing'.
