G.I.G (God Is Good) Exclusive First Listen

Enjoy this exclusive demo recording of our forthcoming single.

Musicians the world over often talk about 'Gigs' without giving much thought to the word itself. It's origins are said to be an acronym for "God Is Good" and was used by blues and jazz musicians in the American South to describe thier working status. (ie. I have work therefore God Is Good) Cumasc Gael's G.I.G (God Is Good) however, tells the lamentable tale of a performer who has sadly and bitterly fallen out of love with his craft...

"...God is good when I'm heading for home!"
