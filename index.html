Debut Album

Cumasc Gael are currently recording their first studio album.

To be realeased in September 2022, 'The Cutty Sark Mutiny' is a true representation of the group's unique, 'Celtic-Bluegrass' sound and features 11 foot-stomping anthems, including the singles: 'Gagged and Bound', 'G.I.G (God is Good) and 'I Said Nothing'.

Cumasc Gael @ Ballyshannon Festival

Cumasc Gael are delighted to confirm they will be appearing at the Ballyshannon Folk Festival this year.
